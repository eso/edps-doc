\subsection{Troubleshooting}

This section provides guidance for diagnosing and resolving common issues encountered during the SPHERE-ZIMPOL Imaging data-reduction cascade.


\subsubsection{Image Incorrectly Rotated / Signal Smeared out}

The task \textbf{zimpol\_coronagraph\_center\_imaging} uses the instrument pipeline recipe \texttt{sph\_zpl\_star\_center\_img}
to identify waffle spots and determine the position of the target behind the coronagraph.
In some cases the detection threshold of 10$\sigma$ is too high and the recipe cannot identify
the target’s position. In such a case the science recipe (\texttt{sph\_zpl\_science\_imaging})
will rotate the frames around an incorrect position which will smear out the signal.
If you see such behaviour you may try to reduce \texttt{star\_center\_img.sigma} to 5.
