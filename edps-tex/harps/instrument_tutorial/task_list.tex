\section{List of workflow tasks}

This is the list of all the tasks and associated recipes in the
\instname\ workflow.  Only some of them are needed for scientific
reduction, they are indicated by the flag "yes" (triggered by default)
or "optional" (triggered only if requested by a workflow
parameter). Other tasks are not used for scientific reduction (they
are indicated by the flag "no"), they are mainly used for instrument
monitoring and they can be executed only by specifying them as
target. Note that, when a task is specified as target, all the tasks
that generate the calibrations needed for it are automatically
executed.

\hline
\textbf{TASK} & \textbf{RECIPE} & \textbf{Used in science reduction} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\hline

contamination\_fp    & espdr\_cal\_contam          & yes & Computes mutual contamination of fibres with Fabry-Perot spectra\\
contamination\_thar  & espdr\_cal\_contam          & yes & Computes mutual contamination of fibres with Th-Ar spectra\\
dark                 & espd\_mdark                 & no  & Computes masterdark and hot pixel table\\
detector\_monitoring & detmon\_opt\_lg\_mr         & no  & Computes detector linearity\\
efficency            & espdr\_cal\_eff\_ab         & no  & Computes fibres relative efficiency\\
flux\_calibration    & espdr\_cal\_flux            & yes & Computes flux calibration\\
lamp\_flats          & espdr\_mflat                & yes & Computes flat-fielding and blaze-function corrections\\
led\_flats           & espdr\_led\_ff              & yes & Computes bad pixels mask \\

order\_definition    & espdr\_orderdef             & yes & Traces the orders on the detector\\
wave\_thar\_fp       & espdr\_wave\_TH\_drift      & yes & Performs one step of the wavelength calibration\\
