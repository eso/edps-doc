\subsection{Exporting the final products}
\label{sec:export_final_products}


\input{../common/common_export}
 
 
Exported products are organized by `DATASET` (named as the first
scientific exposure of the dataset), and `TIMESTAMP` (time of start of
reduction).

The ESPRESSO workflow processes individual science observations, generating a separate product for each raw input file, but also combines together spectra from the same observing template (task {\tt combine\_science}).

The final products saved in the specified directory are: 


\begin{itemize}
\item SPECTRUM\_COMBINED followed by the target name
\item SPECTRUM\_ followed by the exposure identifier (header keyword 'arcfile'). They are located in a subdirectory of the combined spectrum.
\end{itemize}
