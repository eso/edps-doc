Exported products are organized by `DATASET` (named as the first
scientific exposure of the dataset), and `TIMESTAMP` (time of start of
reduction)

The final products saved in the specified directory are: 


\begin{itemize}
\item IMAGE\_{\tt arcfile}. Reduce image corresponding to the exposure specified by the {\tt arcfile} keyword, imaging workflow only.
\item  CATALOG\_{\tt arcfile}. Catalogue of detected sources in IMAGE\_{\tt arcfile}, imaging worlflow only.
\item SPECTRUM\_MOS\_{\tt arcfile}. Reduced spectra extracted from the exposure specified by  the {\tt arcfile} keyword, spectroscopic workflow only.
\end{itemize}
